In your learning sets, brainstorming ideas for your inquiry.

Use the forum which is provided in this area. You can also use the chat if you want, but please post your conclusions to the forum as well.

It is important that you work as a "critical friends", that is offer constructive ideas about how someone might improve others' inquiry. In particular ask yourself the questions

  • Ore they being over ambitious in scope?

  • Is the suggested inquiry something that they have sufficient control over to take an action?

  • Are there any standards or professional requirements that you think might be useful?
