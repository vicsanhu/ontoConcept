Description of the Unit:


  1. The student has to  read the following text: http://cvc.cervantes.es/aula/lecturas/avanzado/lectura_12/texto/
  2. The student has to create a composition. The composition has to continue the story of Eduardo and Eva.
  3. The student submit his/her composition to the peer-student
  4. The peer-student check the submission of the student
  5. The peer-student add his/her own remarks
  6. The student read the first remarks and can provide some feedback (critic, complain, remark...). 
  7. Taking into account the comments of the peer-student, the student can check his document and submit the reviewed composition for the teacher.
  8. The teacher grades the text, taking into account student´s remarks, and assign a qualificationa and a numerical mark. The teacher closes this activity
  9. Finally,the student read his dossier and can also have a look to the marks of others
