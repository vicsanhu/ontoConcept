Submitting the first draft to your learning facilitator

By week two of the module you need to be ready to share your first draft with your learning facilitator. In the text of the draft you should highlight and comment on any issues which you would particularly like feedback on. Dont hold back as if you are having real problems, this is a good opportunity to address them. Note: you must rename each draft you send with a version number i.e. mydraft_version1.doc, then the next time mydraft_version2.doc
